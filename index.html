About Me

As a student at Lapu-Lapu City College who's taking up Bachelor of Industrial Technology — Computer Technology. I grew up curious about how things work, especially technology, which is what led me to study IT and web development.

One of the most rewarding parts of my IIT journey has been learning HTML. Through hands-on practice I learned how simple tags and structure form the backbone of every website — headings, paragraphs, lists, links, images, and semantic elements. Building my own pages helped me understand how content should be organized for both users and accessibility.

I know learning isn’t always easy — I’ve faced failures, stress, and self-doubt — but those challenges made me more resilient. Today I’m driven by the goal of becoming an IT professional who builds useful, creative systems. Learning HTML showed me I can create and control the web — and I’m excited to keep learning CSS, JavaScript, and more.
